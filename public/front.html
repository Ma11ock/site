This site is a celebration of old computers and technology (or, what is old and nostalgic to me). Everything here is designed to look like a late 2000's or early 2010's Linux desktop since that was the last time you could still feel 90's era optimism about technology (or the future in general).

In current year it's impossible to feel anything but disgust and disappointment over the state of technology. Social media has enabled conformism in a way never before thought possible. Every electronic device is a government surveillance machine. Unix is still using X11. Perhaps the Vi guy was right to go full AnPrim.

But this site at www.ryanmj.xyz is not about that. Instead, this is a place to LARP and pretend it's still 2008.

This website is dedicated to mankind.
